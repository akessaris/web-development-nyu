About

Hi, I'm Alex Kessaris, and I need your help to Fund My Extravagancies! You see, there are so many things that I'd like to do in this life, yet I'm constrained by lack of time and (more importantly) a lack of money. Therefore, in a time of modern beggary via media outlets like gofundme, Kickstarter, and even fundmytravel, I figured that I could use this site as a ploy to have people finance my extravagancies!
